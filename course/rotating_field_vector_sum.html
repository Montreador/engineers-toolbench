What to watch: each coloured vector only ever grows and shrinks along its own fixed axis — none of them rotate. Yet their sum (crimson) keeps a constant length of 1.5× peak and sweeps steadily round the dashed circle. That turning sum is the field the rotor chases. Change f and it sweeps faster or slower; that is the entire premise of the drive.
